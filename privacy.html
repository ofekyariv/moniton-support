Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Overview

Moniton ("the App") is a taxi fare calculator for Israeli taxis. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

  • Location data: Used only while the app is in the foreground to calculate taxi fares in real-time. We do not store or transmit your location to our servers.
  • Advertising data: We use Google AdMob which may collect device identifiers for personalized advertising. You can opt out via the App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns. This data is anonymized and aggregated.

How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate taxi fares based on your current location
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To improve app performance and user experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by our advertising and analytics partners (Google AdMob, Firebase) as described above.

Data Retention

Location data is processed locally on your device and is not retained after the app session ends. Analytics data is retained per Google's standard retention policies.

Your Rights

You can disable location access and tracking at any time through your device settings. You can also request deletion of your data by contacting us.
